Tabletop Dice Roller

I play a lot of tabletop games like Dungeons and Dragons, and these games involve a lot of dice rolling to determine whether or not something your character does is a success, a failure, or a critical hit/miss. Since there's no single system of what dice to roll or what the numbers mean, it can be hard to find a good dice rolling program for your particular ruleset. I also got tired of rolling my dice off of tables/desks/other flat surfaces and having to keep manual track of all the results. That's why I made a program that lets the user define their own rules/sets of dice, and tells you how many successes, failures and criticals that you get for you.
